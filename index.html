About Me

As a former continuous improvement professional, I love the opportunities I get to be creative and problem solve as a Software Engineer. The planning stages of a project, web application, or even just back-end routes are as much fun as actually implementing them and I can't lose time any more easily than when there is a bug to fix or a new API to implement. I love solving thorny problems. Breaking down complex features into manageable parts, methodically working through tasks on a scrum board, a mandatory love of learning, and being able to visualize complex patterns related to a thing you can neither see or touch. I spent the last 10 years working in supply chain and continuous improvement roles before revamping my lifestyle and finding my passion in creating beautiful, logical full-stack applications. I am excited to work with, and am proficient in, JavaScript, Python, React, Redux, Express, Flask, Docker, HTML, CSS, Node.Js, SQL, PostgreSQL, OOP, and REST-ful APIs.

I also love to travel... Seen below in the Scottish Highlands w/ my wife.
